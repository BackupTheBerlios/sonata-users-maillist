13 May 2006; Diego Pettenò <flameeyes at gentoo.org>
+files/taglib-1.4-dirtypointer.patch, +taglib-1.4-r1.ebuild:
Add a patch to avoid a dirty pointer in TagLib::FLAC::FilePrivate::tag, that
might lead to crashes if dereferenced.
