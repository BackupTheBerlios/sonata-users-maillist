I have just made a change to the current ctrl-j search filter, and I
thought I'd mention it to those who make use of it (and those who
helped develop it!). As of the latest commit, ctrl-j is no longer used
to toggle open the filter; instead, the custom filter is used as soon
as you start typing instead of the crappy built-in gtk search.

The reasons for this are:
1. Many people didn't know that the ctrl-j filter even exists; now it
should be more prominent.
2. People who type and get the gtk interactive search would be
discouraged by it's lack of searching ability and features.

I'd love to get testing and feedback on it. And definitely let me know
if the search filter ever incorrectly shows up on a keystroke (i.e. it
should never show up for keystrokes like f5 or ctrl-a) or if it
doesn't show up when it should. I think I got all the modifiers and
such covered, but I wouldn't place bets on it ;-)
