As an example of writing lyrics plug-ins, here's one that currently 
works for lyricwiki (btw, the domain name change to lyrics.wikia.com was 
news to me), based on patches by others:

http://tuukka.iki.fi/tmp/lyricwiki2.py

Usage: for use with SVN version of Sonata, download and save next to 
other plugins (gajim_tune.py, localmpd.py, test.py), or in 
~/.sonata/plugins, or in /usr/local/lib/sonata/plugins. Restart Sonata, 
go to Preferences->Plugins, disable "Built-in" version of LyricWiki, 
enable "0.0.1" version of LyricWiki.
