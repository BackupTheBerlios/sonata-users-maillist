Thanks for looking into this, I'm surprised that both bmpx and amarok
use a manual approach. Personally, the manual approach seems like a
pain (both for the user and to implement). I'm still tempted to try
some sort of automated approach to try to hit the vast majority of
cases and keep things very simple..

For what it's worth, assuming that one has their library well
organized, they could use a recent svn feature to save images to
/foo/song_dir/cover.jpg (for example) instead of
~/.covers/artist-album.jpg. If all of the VA songs are in the same
dir, they would all use the same artwork.
