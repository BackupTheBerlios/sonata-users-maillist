On Tue, Dec 23, 2008 at 4:21 AM, Petr Mrazek <peterix at gmail.com> wrote:

> Hi,
> I have a setup like this: all my music is on a samba share, mounted
> as ~/music locally.
> MPD runs locally.
>
> Now, let's say I want to edit tags on a few songs that are in
> ~/music/new/fresh_tracks
>
> After I'm done editing, sonata instructs MPD to update the whole
> database. This can take up to an hour, because I have tons of music
> files.
>

What version of Sonata and mpd are you using? Sonata definitely tells mpd to
only update the songs that were brought up through the tag editor, and I'm
pretty sure it's always been this way.

On the other hand, if you issue a Ctrl-U or choose 'update' from the library
menu, a full update will be called. That's a default I plan on changing for
the next release, but for the behavior you described, sonata should work as
intended.
