> Error grabbing key 162, 0x824dc10
> Error grabbing key 164, 0x824dc10
>
>
> How do I figure out what's what these keys are and why Sonata is
> unable to "grab" them?


The mmkeys extension is trying to grab your multimedia keys on your
keyboard, but some other piece of software is already using them. Are you
using gnome? Newer versions of gnome use dbus to grab the keys, but I think
mmkeys try anyway - and they are needed for people not using gnome or for
older versions of gnome. That's my understanding of the situation anyway,
but it's admittedly a little foggy.

Do your multimedia keys work correctly in Sonata?
