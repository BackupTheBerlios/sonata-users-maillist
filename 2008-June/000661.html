Liviu can chime in with his own opinion, but I don't think this is what he's
referring to. Queueing is a pretty specific feature found in many players
that allows you to queue up multiple songs without having to modify the
playlist. I wouldn't characterize adding a song from your library to the
position after the currently playing song as queueing. For what it's worth,
this is what the feature looked like in Sonata:

http://sonata.berlios.de/images/sonata-queue.png

Notice the "Q" column, in which the song with the 1 will play after the
current song, then the song with the 2, and then back to track 2 in the
playlist. The songs can be dequeued as well.
