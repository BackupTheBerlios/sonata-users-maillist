On 5/26/07, Florian Zeitz <florian.zeitz at gmx.de> wrote:
> Just out of interest: Does sonata actually use a feature that is
> exclusive to gtk.statusicon? I can't seem notice a single difference in
> behaviour.

It's the other way around. The egg.trayicon allows Sonata to use
middle-click for play/pause toggle, mousewheel up/down to raise/lower
the volume, and show a custom mouseover window. In the case of the
gtk.statusicon, the latter can be hackishly worked around by
constantly polling for the user's mouse, but the former two cannot be
done at all.

If the gtk.statusicon, at the very least, supported callbacks for
other mouse button presses (not simply left-click and right-click),
then I would happily get rid of the egg.trayicon.
