I just discovered that I made two mistakes (they don't manifest as bugs,
but still).
First there is a unnecessary space in line 6420 after
settingsDaemonInterface:
"settingsDaemonInterface .connect_to_signal('MediaPlayerKeyPressed',
self.mediaPlayerKeysCallback)"

Second the whole block in __init__ I added should be before
Base.__init__ is called, because otherwise HAVE_MMKEYS has not been set
to False and mmkeys is initialized even if it shouldn't be.
