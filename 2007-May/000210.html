On 5/5/07, intrigeri <intrigeri at boum.org> wrote:
> This padding thing is meant as a feature by the people who develop
> such tagging tools : they say it saves time on next tag edit.
> It's doubtful they will remove what they call a feature just because
> some other people call it a bug.

Interesting, I wasn't aware of this. Indeed, MPD would be the
preferable place to file this bug (feature request?), but MPD
development moves slower than a snail's pace. If some tag editing
tools are padding tags despite the user's intent, then yes, it'd be
nice to strip the spaces. I plan on doing a Sonata release tonight or
tomorrow, so this isn't going to make it in it; especially since it
isn't necessarily trivial and there is potential for regressions. I'll
put it on the todo for the 1.2 release though.

For what it's worth, I tested gmpc, xmms, and bmp, and all three
(granted gmpc is still mpd) show the trailing spaces in the tag. But I
don't know what media players people use these days though..
