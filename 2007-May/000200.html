> For clarification, what are you editing the tag with? Taglib through

> have the bug filed (unless you're using taglib and somehow Sonata is

I'm using Sonata tag edit function, i.e. Taglib. BTW, it does not seem
consistent in respect to padding or not : I've not isolated testcases,
but I recently saw it edit a tag without padding it with spaces.

> There very well might be reasons that someone wants leading or
> trailing spaces in their tag, I'm not convinced that mpd or sonata
> should override this..

This padding thing is meant as a feature by the people who develop
such tagging tools : they say it saves time on next tag edit.
It's doubtful they will remove what they call a feature just because
some other people call it a bug.

A lot of tagging tools do so, so I guess a lot of people already have
music collections mixing padded tags and non-padded ones, and just
don't realize it at all because their music player hides such
trailing space. This is my case : mpd is the first music player I'm
using that displays tags trailing spaces... and I've tried quite a lot
other players, which makes me think that most tag reading
libraries/bindings hide them.

To end with, I can't find any reason someone could want trailing
spaces in a tag ; this obviously does not mean that none exist, but
this seems to me as a weird usage case, compared to the one I'm
talking of.
