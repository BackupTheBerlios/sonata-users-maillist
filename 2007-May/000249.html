Umlauts have always been and still are a pain in computing for encoding
reasons - locally they cause grief sometimes and since about a year they told
everybody they work in the domain name system. The truth simply is - that
is not true. My advice is don't use them on the net and avoid them locally in
file names too - over the years I have seen many stupid malfunctions caused
by umlauts.
