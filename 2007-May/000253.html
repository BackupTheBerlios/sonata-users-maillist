> 3. My Vorbis Various Artists albums are tagged like this:
>
> User comments section follows...
>         ARTIST=2Pac & Snoop Dogg
>         TITLE=2 Of Amerikaz Most Wanted
>         ALBUM=Live At The House Of Blues Soundtrack
>         DATE=2005
>         TRACKNUMBER=25
>         GENRE=Rap
>
> So the artist changes throughout the album. Problem is Sonata can't find
> the covers anymore, because there's no album by the artist "Pac & Snoop
> Dogg" called "Live At The House Of Blues Soundtrac". Amazon doesn't
> like the artist "Various Artists" either. Finally "2Pac" turned up the
> proper result. If it was the search term I could easily live with that,
> but because the artist changes with every track I'd have to search the
> image every time it does. Is there some way for Sonata to check for
> Various Artists albums and treat their cover names differently?

Are you sure there are multiple artists for this album name? A VA
album is when there are different songs from an album by different
artists, not when every song is by multiple artists (2pac and Snoop),
which seems to be the scenario you're referring to. That's what my
quick search suggests anyway:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tupac:_Live_at_the_House_of_Blues

Regardless, it should be handled correctly either way. If it truly is
a VA album, you only have to set the album art once (in version 1.1).
If instead the artist is 2pac and Snoop for every song, that should
also be handled just fine by Sonata, as it's the typical scenario.
