Hello,

context : editing the playlist with the keyboard (Sonata SVN r663)

what I’m doing : I navigate with the Up and Down arrows, focus a given
track, then press the DEL key.

what I’m expecting to happen : the selected track is removed from the
playlist, and the keyboard focus moves to the first remaining next
track.

what is happening instead : the selected track is removed from the
playlist, and the keyboard focus is « reset » ; I mean : no track is
selected/highlighted anymore ; pressing one of the Down and Up arrows,
then, moves the focus to the first track in the playlist.

In particular, this is annoying when editing large playlists with
the keyboard.

Feel free to consider this as a minor bug ; the positive fact is that
the only remaining annoying things I can find in Sonata are of this
kind :)
