There is a sonata patch floating around to add real transparency support to
Sonata - it's been mentioned on this list before. I haven't applied it
because it's silly to have every application  patched rather than it be
supported somewhere upstream. For example:
http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=516885

As for images in the tabs, I'd be open to having a hidden setting in the
sonatarc for this. You should also be aware that you can hide tabs
completely (if you don't use some, like playlists or streams) by
right-clicking on the tabs. Perhaps this is sufficient for what you want
(reducing horizontal space of tabs?).
