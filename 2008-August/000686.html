Hi,
I would first thank you about this wonderful software, it's simple & 
powerful.

I'm here to ask for tabs without images, because images take too much 
space, and it spends space.
You can do two things to solve this:
 - Make smaller images
 - Remove them
Of course, I ask to add an option, not to disable it totally (;

I would also want to point you something very interesting: the RGBA 
transparency.
It is made by Murrine, and it's very beautiful and easy to enable in 
your program. You can see the blog post 
<http://www.cimitan.com/blog/2007/12/12/gtk-rgba-transparent-widgets-with-the-murrine-engine/> 
and the call for coders (which shows how to integrate it in theory) but 
for Gtk. 
<http://www.cimitan.com/blog/2008/02/17/rgba-colormap-by-default-in-gtk-call-for-a-coder/>

You can find the Emesene patch <http://emesene.org/trac/ticket/418> 
(which is written in Python) to know how to integrate it in Sonata.

Thank you, and good bye!
Othmane Benkirane.
