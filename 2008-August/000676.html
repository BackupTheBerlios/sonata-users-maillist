On Tue, Jul 29, 2008 at 2:13 PM, Twigathy <twigathy at gmail.com> wrote:

> What needs changing is for the volume control to be configurable as to
> which mixer thingy it changes. Or for someone to go "Hey you fool,
> there's setting oojamaflip in /etc/obscure.conf!" ;-)


Since mpd does all of the audio handling, the setting is not client
specific. The mixer can be specified in your mpd.conf.

#mixer_type                      "alsa"
#mixer_device                    "default"
#mixer_control                   "PCM"
