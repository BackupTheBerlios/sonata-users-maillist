Hello John,
yes MPD (Music Playin Daemon) is in Ubuntu repositories as well.
Search for a package named mpd in Synaptic. You will need to install
and set it up to play music with Sonata. MPD is the music server - it
handles the music library and actual playing. Sonata is one of many
(but it is my very favourite :-) ) players (frontends) that
communicate with MPD and lets you control the music played.

Sonata will not play without MPD.
