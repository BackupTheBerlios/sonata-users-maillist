> > Looking at the code, the criterion seems to be 4 minutes or half of the

> > restarts.

> Correct, and this is what the audioscrobbler protocol states. The
> other players are not doing it correctly if they submit info at 4
> minutes or halfway.
>
> From http://www.audioscrobbler.net/development/protocol/
> "1. The track must be submitted once it has finished playing. Whether
> it has finished playing naturally or has been manually stopped by the
> user is irrelevant.
> 2. The track must have been played for a duration of at least 240
> seconds or half the track's total length, whichever comes first.
> Skipping or pausing the track is irrelevant as long as the appropriate
> amount has been played."
>
