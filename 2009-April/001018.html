I wish setting up Sonata was a breeze, but as it is, Sonata depends on 
MPD, which depends on editing a text file, which is a basic computer skill.

> Surely, as the two come 
> together, they are part of each other, so it is a part of Sonata, 
> because of that reason. 

Everything more or less comes together in the computer world. However, 
the best place to ask for support depends on various aspects:

* The best people to help in issues specific to Ubuntu are the Ubuntu 
people.

* The best people to explain basic computer/Linux/Gnome/Ubuntu skills 
are people who've learned them recently themselves and can empathise.

* Sonata is not useful without MPD, so we want people to get their MPD 
problems solved :-)

Installing packages is specific to Ubuntu, as is the exact version and 
configuration of Gnome etc. ubuntuforums.org is a particularly 
beginner-welcoming community for asking your questions.
