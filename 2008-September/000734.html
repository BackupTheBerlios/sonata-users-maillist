In theory, real-time search for the library would be a good thing, I'm just
worried that it could be quite a bit slower than the playlist searching. For
one, people can have very large libraries while their playlists are
typically much smaller. Secondly, this searching requires calling mpd and
returning results for each letter change (which could be slow over a
network), while the playlist searching is just filtering based on info that
has already been retrieved from mpd.

But if another mpd client does it and hasn't had complaints, I suppose it
can't be too bad. I'll look into implementing this. Since I run mpd on
localhost and don't have a huge library, I'd like it personally :)
