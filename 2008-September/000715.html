On 14-Sep-08, 13:54 (CDT), John <jakewc2 at sky.com> wrote: 
> What about updates and the svn, is it worth installing the software and  
> keeping up to date that way with the nightly builds?

I'm not Scott, and he may well have his own opinions, but if you have a
working sonata and MPD, don't mess with the nightly builds or updates
unless you want to develop Sonata. Scott does a good job, but bugs do
get checked into SVN (usually without appropriate log messages :-)) and
things do occasionally break. That's the way things are running the
development branch, so if all you want is a working Sonata, and you have
one, don't mess with things.

All IMHO, of course.
