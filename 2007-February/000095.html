On 2/2/07, Jon Temple <jontemple at gmail.com> wrote:
> I have various artist albums. Whenever I change my library view to "artists"
> I see many artists with only one or two songs in my library. I would really
> only like to see artists that have full albums in that view. I think it
> would be much better if all albums with more than one contributing artist
> were categorized under "various artists" or something similar to that.

I don't think I agree with this. In my opinion, artist view should
show just that: a listing of artists. While there are some users that
would be happy with what you describe, others may, in fact, want to
find a specific artist from a VA album. I personally don't think it
makes sense to try to accommodate one type of user when the other type
is the expected action.

IMO, you should switch to album or filesystem view for a VA album.
