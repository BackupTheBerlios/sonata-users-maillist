Hello,

this is a kind request from a lame python coder ;-)

I often need to switch the output of mpd, switching between primary
ALSA output when I am home, the icecast output when I am at work and
sometimes having both. "mpc" has functions "outputs" to display outputs
with alias names, "enable" and "disable" to control that. I wish to have
something like that in the Sonata menu, maybe even a bit more clever:

-> Select One Output -> "Alsa Home" []
|                   \-> "IceCast output" [x]
\-> Disable Output -> ...
\-> Enable Output -> ...

So the idea is when you click in "Select One Output" it enables that one
chosen output and disables all others. And with Disable and Enable, you
can choose and enable/diable certain outpus selectively.

Second thing I wish to have is the quick search and jump function for
the playlist. Someone posted a patch already to implement that.

And the third thing is a pre-queue function, like in xmms (q button) or
one of those pympd plugins. However, this function is a bit ugly to
implement, it could be done in two ways, by rearranging the playlist
entries (however the playlist is modified then and it only works without
shuffle), or by manually pushing of the playlist: permanent polling of
the mpd status and jumping to a prescheduled song right in the moment
when the previos songs finish.

Ideally, search&jump could be combined with pre-queue, like it's done in
XMMS.

Eduard.
