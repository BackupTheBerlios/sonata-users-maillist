2009/8/12 Shashi <connect2shashi at gmail.com>:
> Dear list,
> I am switching from non-free services to free alternatives, libre.fm
> is a free alternative for last.fm, it will be very supportive if
> sonata extended its scrobbling support to it.
>
> Are there already any plugins that already accomplish this?
>
> Best,
> Shashi
> _______________________________________________
> Sonata-users mailing list
> Sonata-users at lists.berlios.de
> https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/sonata-users
>

Disable scrobbling in Sonata, install mpdscribble [1] and configure
/etc/mpdscribble.conf to scrobble to last.fm and/or libre.fm.
mpdscribble.conf must be readable for the user (not only by root).

[1] http://mpd.wikia.com/wiki/Client:Mpdscribble
