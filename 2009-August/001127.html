As of August 2nd, 2009 lyricwiki is no longer able to provide
programmatic access to their collection of lyrics[1]. This means you
will get a message in the Info tab describing the situation and pointing
you to the lyrics page for the currently playing song.

Anybody know another service that can be used instead?

Note: this will only affect songs which don't have lyrics already saved
in the location specified in your preferences.
