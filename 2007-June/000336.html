I definitely like the former idea, no need to poll amazon for cover
art over and over if it isn't found once.

As for the latter idea, resetting the cover art is really akin to
deleting the artwork file being used (either in ~/.covers/ or
/home/music/foo/etc/). There isn't much problem with deleting the
artwork if it was found in ~/.covers, since that should mean it was
automatically downloaded by Sonata, but for artwork in their music
directory, we don't know if it came from Sonata or not. So I guess, in
this instance, the right thing to do is make the user confirm that
they really wish to delete that artwork file...

I'm going to implement the former idea right now, and think some more
about the other.
