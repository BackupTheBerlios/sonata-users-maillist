For what it's worth, it appears that cover art will work fine when the
http_proxy environment variable is set. Unfortunately, lyrics are
impossible to get working because ZSI apparently doesn't support it
[1][2]. I still need to look at if it's possible for audioscrobbler
support, but since I didn't write that, I'm not particularly inclined
to want to muck around too much.

And to intrigeri: Sonata will indeed never become a gnome application
or gnome dependent, no worries there ;) And no, clients aren't able to
query proxy info from mpd; nor is parsing the mpd.conf a suitable
solution since it can very well be on a different computer than the
client.
