Hi Scott,
I wrote too soon: bug.
Within Album view, when adding or replacing an album with the same
name as another album, all instances of the album name will load.
Example:
Playing Schoenberg something or other, I select the Library tab then
select Album view. I right click on "Tristan und Isolde (Leonard
Bernstein)" and select Replace from the popup menu. Back in the
Current tab, we find that all three versions of Tristan have been
loaded (Bernstein, Karajan & Furtwängler).

I should add that this sort of thing is where Quodlibet excels, in
that it can even differentiate between masterings of the same
recording. (The great 1961 Solti recording of Strauss' Salome is a
good example of this, with an original 1990 CD and a superior 2006
remastered CD).

Best,
--Fergus
