Hi Tomas,
Thanks for the tip.
Not quite what I had in mind, you're right.
The trouble with tooltips is that they're slow to appear, the text is
small and has to read in detail.
My idea of UI heaven - that Sonata is close to achieving - is to be
whipping around an application without a mouse, and without waiting
for delayed info to appear.
(The key thing here is that you should be able to dynamically narrow
down the number of, say, albums your'e looking at, until a match is
met. Or not.)
Not that I'm against toolips: a sensible implementation for a media
player would include audio - as in Nautilus.
--Fergus
