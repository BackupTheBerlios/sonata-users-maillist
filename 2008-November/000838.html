bonkers that when I do a search for Salome in Album view (or any view)
it returns the contents of all those 5 versions. I suppose the
equivalent would be to do a Google search for something (Salome) and
have the contents of the pages displayed, rather than the titles and
meta data.

suggestion of which version of "Siehe, der Tag ist Nähe" I'm looking
at amongst the five possibilities. But there is none.

version of "Fake Plastic Trees" is identical. That is, it is exactly
