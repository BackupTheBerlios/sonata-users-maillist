Hello,

since it seems to be the time of great collection views rewrite, here
are my two cents.

Thanks to the new Album view, one of my current use-cases is: I'm
mainly using the Album view, and I happen to see e.g.
    "In Rainbows (Radiohead, 2007)"
Then I think hey, why not listen to Radiohead, but I'd prefer
another album.

Currently, I have to switch to Artist view manually, find Radiohead in
the list and so on. IMHO, this lacks hyperlinking and
transverse navigation.

What I'd love to be enabled to do is: the "Radiohead" word, in the
Album view extract mentioned above, could be some kind of link.
Clicking on it could switch to Artist view, and lead me directly
inside the "Radiohead" pseudo-folder.

I guess it's not so hard to implement, but the tricky UI part is: in
Album view, the whole album list item already has a defined behavior
when clicked; can the artist part become a link on its own? I don't
know if GTK allows this.

Any thoughts on this?
