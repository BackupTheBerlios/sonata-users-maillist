Scott,
I'm blown away - many thanks!
This makes a huge difference: a significant improvement in usability.
(As of tonight I default entirely to Sonata).

The only odd thing is the search: so, I'm looking at my albums and I
just want to see those titled "Tristan und Isolde", I select 'Album'
from the search mode drop down and begin entering 'tristan...' in a
flash I get results (wonderfully responsive), but instead of showing
the albums it shows the songs within those albums. Which means I get a
song list from all three versions of Tristan. Odd, no?
Strictly speaking, the expected behaviour would be to return a result
set based upon the current view.
Further, were I in Artist list view and searched for 'Album'
"tristan", I would see a list of the three artists in my collection
with Tristan und Isolde recordings. And so on.
In this respect, Sonata is trying to do too much. It needn't.
--
I know this might be pushing it but an album cover preview would be
perfect, in place of the generic (though attractive) blue icon. But
you know that already...

In general, as far as UI goes, Sonata is easily the best of the single
pane players - fast, intuitive, discrete.
But for a few details, almost perfect.

Again, much thanks - great work.

> I have to admit that it amazes me a bit, but there was an album mode like
> you described in older versions of Sonata. I took it out and replaced it
> with another mode that I thought much superior - but clearly there are many
> people that just want an album view cutting cross all artists/genres. It's
> been on my todo for a while to bring it back and you finally gave me the
> motivation to work on it today. So do a svn update and try it out. There's
> room for improvement (it doesn't yet show album art as I will need to
> refactor that, and its Various Artists logic isn't as good as the VA logic
> elsewhere yet), but it does have some simple logic for lumping together,
> say, classical albums into a single VA album. I also took the liberty of
> making some improvements (IMO) to the other modes, including more details
> and caching.
>
> Suggestions welcome. Aside from some of the pending improvements I mentioned
> above, I'm tempted to switch to more contextual information for some of
> these views. For example, under the listing of artists, it'd be nice to say
> the number of albums for each artist - although I'm not sure whether to
> replace the current information with it or add it to the current info.
>
> As for handling composer tags, I'd suggest filing a feature request for
> this.
