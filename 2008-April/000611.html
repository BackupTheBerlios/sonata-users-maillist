This is a problem on lyricwiki.org's side. I posted the problem on
their site and it appears that they fixed it - see
http://lyricwiki.org/User_talk:Sean_Colombo#Redirect_in_SOAP_calls.3F.
You'll have to delete any lyrics files (from ~/.lyrics) in order to
get Sonata to requery the lyrics.
