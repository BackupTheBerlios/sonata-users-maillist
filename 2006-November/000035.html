Sonata uses python's distutils to install, and believe it or not,
distutils does not provide a mechanism for uninstalling. Search this
on google and you'll find many people asking the same question.

The best thing to do is to install Sonata through your package manager
and then you can uninstall the same way. Otherwise, the only other
option is manual*.

Scott
