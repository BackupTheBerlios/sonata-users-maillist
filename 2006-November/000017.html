Hi,

I just discovered Sonata today and I really like it!  I love its
intelligent, clean, minimalistic interface. Very nice, well done.

There are few things that feel missing to me though. Here are a few
suggestions:

* Popup the currently playing song notification when un-pausing. I use
  key bindings that call mpc to play/pause and when I come back from a long
  break and hit play, it would be nice to know what's playing.

* Some sort of play/pause indication in the tray icon would be nice,
  although not really necessary I guess.

* Preserve aspect ratio when scaling the album art. Not all album art is
  square.

* Display the year of release in the popup album art window.

* Share cover art with gmpc/pympd (in ~/.covers IIRC)

Thanks for a great app!
