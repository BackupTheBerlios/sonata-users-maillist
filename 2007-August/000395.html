Should be, please report if it's not the case. As for the searchbar, I
have to say that I much prefer the searchbar at the bottom of the
window. I personally hate the way that GTK handles comboboxes at the
bottom. I realize their intent is to preserve the selected item and
having it scroll is the only way to accommodate that behavior, but it
feels so awkward. Regardless, I don't want to change Sonata just for
this annoyance. Even if you do use a tiling WM, that doesn't force
Sonata to be positioned at the bottom of your screen (and you could
always use a floating window just for Sonata, at least with some
tiling WMs).
