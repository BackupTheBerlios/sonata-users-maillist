On 8/18/07, Thomas Bevan <thomas.bevan at gmail.com> wrote:
> I'm having problems with the remote downloading of album covers.
> Whenever I ask for a remote image I get the response "No Cover Art
> Found" irrespective of the album I look for.

Fetching artwork with older versions of Sonata broke because of
Amazon. Sonata was requesting gzip'ed pages from Amazon if they were
available, and was using them contently. But at some point, these
gzip'ed pages being fetched started to return garbage. So newer
versions of Sonata have the compression requesting turned off.

You can either use version 1.2.2 or remove all instances of
"request.add_header('Accept-encoding', 'gzip')" from your current
version's code.
