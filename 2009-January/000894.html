and started after sonata. Then I changed the playlist to
make sonata consistent again.

a total of 3 mpd servers (local, home-desktop, icecast-source).
When I switch between these, I frequently have to restart sonata
because:
- the playlist gets corrupted with elements from the previous server
- the saved streams dissapear
- the playlists disappear
- rarely sonata hangs in 'not connected' state

or it becomes with a backspace keypress.
