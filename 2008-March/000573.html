this is my first attempt in posting to a list. I hope I did it right.

Thank you very much for this wonderful piece of software. Sonata is indeed  
elegant looking, and it works very well. I was using gmpc before, and  
Sonata has a much better and tidy UI. Everything is where you would  
suppose it to be, and there's nothing really you would miss... still, I  
have an idea how to make it even better. :)

What I propose is a function "send to playlist..." in the context menu. It  
would then be possible to send any track from "current" or "library" to  
any existing playlist (or even a new one) _without_ having to interrupt  
the current listening session. I really think this would add a big deal of  
comfort to my new favourite player. Think of it. In practice, you would be  
able to bookmark your favourite songs as you hear them, compile playlists  
for different occasions or moods, or prepare collections for burning to CD.

Thanks a lot for considering!! -senbei
