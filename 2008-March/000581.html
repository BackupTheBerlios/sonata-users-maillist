Sonata has a menu item in the current playlist tab called 'send list to'
with menu items of 'new playlist' and a list of all your stored playlists.
I'm not crazy about adding another menu item, which may potentially confuse
people about the difference between saving the list and saving selected
items. Perhaps the two could be combined into one menu item or a popup
dialog could be used to simplify the interface.

But anyway, I'm not particularly interested in this feature so I won't be
getting to it soon. I'd recommend posting a feature request at
https://developer.berlios.de/feature/?group_id=7323 and maybe someone else
that likes the idea will create a patch.
