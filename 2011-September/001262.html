Last year, I refactored a bit how Sonata handles the tray icon and in
articular, I removed all those "if HAVE_..." tests and proposed an
unified interface to both egg.trayicon and gtk.StatusIcon. Basically, it
abstracts (maybe in a very adequate way) the use of the tray icon by
Sonata so that the main code doesn't bother to know if it deals with the
egg extension or the built-in GTK thing. I suggest you have a look
there: https://github.com/multani/sonata/commit/dc9427

I had a look at your patch, and it seems it can fit in the proposed
unified tray interface, since AFAIK, the appindicator stuff replaces the
tray icon (I'm not familiar with appindicator, so I may be wrong).  So,
I would strongly encourage you to rebase your patch on the work I did
back then, I think it makes things clearer in the main code.
