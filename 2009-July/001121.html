Hi Martin,

Scrobbling has been working here and I have heard no other reports of people
having problems. If sonata is correctly sending 'now playing' updates, then
it is very weird that normal scrobbles aren't working.

If you would like to debug further, you can look at the
handle_change_status() method in the sonata/self.scrobbler.py file. One of
the self.post() calls should be occurring - for example, if you play more
than 4 minutes of a song and then press stop, the self.post() call on the
last line of the method should be triggered. You can sprinkle some print
statements in there to debug.

Scott
