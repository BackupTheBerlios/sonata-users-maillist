You mean you want to choose the album cover for a song? Well, when
Sonata is playing a song right-click the no cover icon (looks like a
CD and is to the left of the song info) and choose either "Use remote
image" (which will give you what you linked) or "Use local image"
(lets you choose a cover image from your harddrive).

Your songs need to have the metadata defined for at least artist and
album for it to work properly.
