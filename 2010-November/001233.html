We're actually looking for translators. Sonata is translated at ~82%, we

How ? This is very easy, with CodingTeam, we have a very pretty
interface to do it. Check it out : http://codingteam.net/project/sonata/i18n
You just have to choose your language, and then under the English text,
you can translate directly. We'll be very glad if some of you could help
us to do that. I'll do the french translations, but I can not do
anything else.
For any question, as usual, you can come to the jabber room :
